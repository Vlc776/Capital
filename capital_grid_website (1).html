Who We Are

Capital Grid Design Ltd is a creative performing arts company that specializes in artistic expression through African-inspired designs. Our work spans interior decorations, cultural exhibitions, and performing arts events that highlight the beauty, richness, and depth of African heritage.

Based in Stockton-On-Tees, England, our mission is to bring African stories to life using bold visuals, traditional motifs, and artistic experiences that connect the past to the future.
